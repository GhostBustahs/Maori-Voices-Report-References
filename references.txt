HOW THE NUMBERS WERE MADE
Two multilingual sentence-embedding models were evaluated:
  - distiluse-base-multilingual-cased-v2  (referred to as mBERT)
  - paraphrase-multilingual-mpnet-base-v2 (referred to as XLM-R)
For each of 20 te reo Māori terms, the top-10 nearest neighbours were scored
against a literature-grounded Culturally Appropriate Neighbour (CAN) set,
within a controlled 187-term candidate pool.
Results: overall Cultural Semantic Alignment 9.0% and 10.5% vs a 5.7% random
baseline; 18 of 20 highest-similarity retrievals scored CSA = 0.
