How the numbers were made

Two multilingual sentence-embedding models (distiluse-base-multilingual-cased-v2 and paraphrase-multilingual-mpnet-base-v2) were evaluated against a 20-term te reo Māori benchmark. For each term, the top-10 nearest neighbours were scored against a literature-grounded Culturally Appropriate Neighbour set. Overall Cultural Semantic Alignment: 9.0% and 10.5% against a 5.7% random baseline; 18 of 20 highest-similarity retrievals scored zero. Full method and the random-baseline derivation are in the manuscript and the repo above.
